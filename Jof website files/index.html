ABOUT

Current Role - Director Of Business Development - Accelbyte

Holding a degree in Digital Media & Game Design from the Dublin Institute of Technology, I embarked on a diverse career path within the gaming industry. I initially delved into game development before moving to a role at GameSparks Ltd., providing backend solutions built on platforms including Microsoft Azure, AWS, and GCP over five years.



Post-acquisition by AWS, I transitioned into a Technical Specialist role, deepening my understanding of gaming technology. This expertise led me to a position at Microsoft Azure as a cloud specialist for games.

Continuing my journey at Microsoft, I moved to the Havok team, managing developer relationships across EMEA as a Developer Account Manager.

Currently, I occupy the role of Director Of Business Development at Accelbyte, where I leverage my blend of technical knowledge and business acumen to drive strategic growth within the gaming sector.
